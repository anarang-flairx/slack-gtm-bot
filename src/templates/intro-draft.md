**About FlairX**

FlairX is an AI Interview Platform that accelerates hiring by up to 70% through a powerful combination of our **AI Avatar Interviewer** and **Expert Human Interviewer** solutions. Companies can choose the full stack or use individual components depending on their hiring needs.

FlairX handles end-to-end candidate screening, starting from **resume screening,** followed by high-volume **AI voice agent calls** for candidate outreach and initial eligibility checks, and then structured evaluations using our AI Avatar and Expert Human Interviewers.

**1. AI Avatar Interviewer (Automated Structured Interviews)**
Our AI avatar conducts structured interview rounds and can handle hundreds of candidates per day. It:

- Customizes questions and asks intelligent follow-ups in real time
- Analyzes responses and evaluates candidates consistently
- Generates detailed, unbiased reports while masking personal identifiers
- Includes fraud prevention and deepfake detection capabilities

AI Avatar Ziva Interview Recording: [**FlairX_AI Avatar Demo**](https://www.canva.com/design/DAHGngU_YLs/htkf8C93P-Qazdbv8OpF6w/view)

**2. Expert Human Interviewer Platform (White-Glove Evaluation)**
For deeper skill assessments, we offer a white-glove expert interviewer network led by senior industry leaders such as Engineering Directors from Amazon, Product Heads from Google, and other domain experts. This ensures precise evaluation and strong skill-to-role alignment.

**3. Interview Orchestrator (Scheduling Automation)**
FlairX Interview Orchestrator automatically collects candidate availability, syncs with interviewers' Outlook and Google calendars, identifies optimal interview slots, and schedules interviews without recruiter intervention. It removes the manual back-and-forth of scheduling, follow-ups, and rescheduling.
